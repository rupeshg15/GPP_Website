Courses Offered

Courses Offered

The courses are offered in the streams of Engineering and Architecture, Management and Business administration, Science, Arts, Humanity and social sciences.

Vision

To develop self-reliant, versatile, innovative, quality conscious engineers for betterment of society.

Mission

1. Imparting updated curriculum in association with stakeholders. 2. Providing with the state of art infrastructure & facilities. 3. Set up strategic alliance with industries. 4. Enhancing e-governance. 5. Continuous development of faculty & staff.

Postgraduate Programmes

At the Post-Graduate(M.Tech.) level GPP has the following programs:

1. Diploma Computer Science
2. Diploma Information Technology
3. Diploma in Civil Engineering
4. Diploma in Electrical Engineering
5. Diploma in Electronics Engineering
6. Diploma in Metallurgical Engineering
7. Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
8. Diploma in Dress Designing and Manufacturing
